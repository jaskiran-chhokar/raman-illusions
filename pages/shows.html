Parlour Magic

Is usually for an audience of up to 20 to 100 people. The show is usually 30 minutes long and involves a lot of audience participation. From mind reading to, making everyday objects levitate, and then money changing into different currencies and re-appearing in the most unexpected places. This show is guaranteed to entertain audiences of any age.

Perfect for Small Gatherings, Private Parties, Birthday Parties, or Team Building events, as the mood will be set for the rest of day as guests converse over how the tricks may have been done

Stage Show
